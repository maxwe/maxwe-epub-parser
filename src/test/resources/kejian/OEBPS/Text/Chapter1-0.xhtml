Main Means of Transportation in China’s Cities

It’s most convenient to travel in China’s cities by taxi, but it is expensive. It is inexpensive to travel by bus, however it’s not only relatively slow, but also crowded and stops a lot. In recent years, the number of private cars has increased greatly and as a result traffic congestion is a common occurrence. You can avoid traffic congestion by taking the subway. In cities with subways, there are subway stations in most of the busy districts, but the subway is also crowded during rush hour.

China is still “the kingdom of bicycles”. You will see streams of people riding on bicycles on the roads. Most Chinese still use bicycles. However, many people prefer motorcycles and electric bicycles if conditions permit.
