Introduction to the Main Characters

弗雷德（Fúléidé）·史密斯（Shǐmìsī）

Fred Smith, a young American scholar, is researching global urbanization problems. He comes to Beijing to gather data while traveling in China. He is a serious and strict man.

梅西（Méixī）·琼斯（Qióngsī）

Macy Jones, the editor of City Life magazine, is now assisting Fred in his research project on behalf of her press. She is a lively and curious girl. She likes taking photographs.

刘（Liú）　强（Qiáng）

Liu Qiang, a Chinese friend of Fred's elder brother, is working in Beijing. He is very familiar with Beijing and passionate. He is a great help to Fred and Macy as they travel in China.
