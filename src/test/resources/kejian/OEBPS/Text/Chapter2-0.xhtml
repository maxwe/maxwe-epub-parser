Airline Companies in China

There are at present over 20 airline companies in China. Some of the major national airlines are Air China (“Guohang”, abbreviated CA), China Eastern Airlines (Group) (“Donghang”, abbreviated MU), and China Southern Airlines (Group) (“Nanhang”, abbreviated CZ). These airlines fl y not only domestic routes but international routes as well. There are also local airline companies, such as Sichuan Airlines and Hainan Airlines, which mainly fly domestic routes. China’s private airline companies have also developed a lot in recent years. One of these is Spring and Autumn Airline Company, which has introduced lower priced domestic flights.
